Demonslayer

Story about:

Demon Slayer is set in Taishō-era Japan, where demons hunt humans at night and a secret Demon Slayer Corps fights them. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind boy whose family is massacred by demons, with his sister Nezuko turned into one. Determined to cure Nezuko and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps and trains in swordsmanship and breathing techniques. He travels with allies like Zenitsu and Inosuke, facing powerful demons with unique abilities called Blood Demon Arts. Their ultimate enemy is Muzan Kibutsuji, the Demon King who created most demons. The anime is known for beautiful animation, emotional storytelling, and intense fights. Themes include family bonds, empathy for tragic villains, and pushing past one’s limits.
