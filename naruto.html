Naruto

Story about:

Naruto centers on Naruto Uzumaki, an energetic orphan ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village. As a baby, he had the powerful Nine-Tails fox sealed inside him, causing the villagers to fear and shun him. Through training and missions with his teammates Sasuke and Sakura under their teacher Kakashi, Naruto slowly gains strength and recognition. The story follows his growth from troublemaker to hero while dealing with dangerous enemies like the Akatsuki. Friendship, perseverance, and overcoming loneliness are core themes. Major arcs show Naruto trying to save Sasuke from a path of revenge and darkness. The series mixes emotional backstories with large-scale ninja battles and unique jutsu techniques.
