Attack on titan

Story about:

Attack on Titan follows Eren Yeager, who lives in a walled city built to protect humanity from giant humanoid creatures called Titans. After a colossal Titan breaches the outer wall and his mother is killed, Eren vows to exterminate all Titans. He joins the military with his friends Mikasa and Armin to fight back. As the story progresses, they uncover dark secrets about the Titans and the history of their world. The series explores themes of freedom, revenge, and the cycle of hatred. Battles are intense, with vertical maneuvering gear allowing humans to fight Titans in fast aerial combat. The tone is dark and emotional, with frequent twists and heavy character drama.
